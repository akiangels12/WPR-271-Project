About Belgium Campus

Learn about our mission, history, and the beautiful campus that makes Belgium Campus a unique place to study.

Our Mission

At Belgium Campus, our mission is to provide exceptional education and foster a nurturing environment for our students to thrive. We are dedicated to academic excellence and preparing our students for successful careers.

Our History

Founded in 1999, Belgium Campus has grown from a small institution into a leading university known for its innovative programs and commitment to student success. Our history is marked by milestones and achievements that reflect our dedication to education.

Our Values

To transform South Africa into a prosperous nation, higher education must play a crucial role by producing highly skilled graduates. Our strategy focuses on making a significant impact on regional development through collaboration with industry and business, ensuring we deliver a competent workforce.

Our Goal

Our goal is to supply the industry with highly qualified IT professionals through high-quality, practice-oriented education and training of an international standard, contributing to economic growth and wealth creation.
